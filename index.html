Educational Background

I completed both my 10th board examination and higher secondary education at Techno India Group Public School, under the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). In my class 10 examination, I studied a diverse range of subjects including English, Science, Mathematics, Bengali (2nd language), Social Studies (which encompassed History, Civics, Economics, and Geography), and Information Technology (IT). For my class 12 studies, my subjects were English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. After completing my higher secondary education, I appeared for the West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEE) and successfully secured a place at the Institute of Engineering and Management (IEM), New Town, where I am currently pursuing my bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering.
